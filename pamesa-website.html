Pamesa Redesign

Web Design

Improve the visual design and conversion of Pamesa 's website.

Pamesa 's website contains usability problems in different sections in conjunction with broken links and an outdated visual design unlike its competitors.
Areas
Research, Ideation, Visual Design, UI,UX.
Duration
1 week

Highlights

Goal
Design a website that improves conversion and usability, when looking for interior design inspiration in Pamesa.
Site problem
The site has poor overall usability, with long interactions, too many options on screen at once and no clear goal defined.
Site problem
The flows within the website are not clear, and generate confusion when interacting with the product.
Site problem
The on-site experience does not give you the same sensations as visiting the main distributor.
Site problem
The overall visual design of the site feels outdated compared to interior design agencies or direct competitors.
Main challenge
How might we present the content of the site in a way that inexperienced users can understand?

Redesigning a problem

The idea of the redesign
It all starts playing FIFA 23 with Villareal. While I was looking at his shirt and "el estadio de la ceramica ", I was interested in looking for his website and observing the quality of his work.
But... the site had problems
I soon realized that the site had serious usability problems. Long animations and broken links.
Complicated navigation
In addition to having a navigation that changed and was not consistent between different screens.
Direct and indirect competition
At a cursory glance, several of the direct and indirect competitors had a more professional design.

Understanding needs and problems

Interviews and avatars of users with jobs to be done were conducted to understand their behavior and actions.
User journey
The path from finding the inspiration to contacting the company for the purchase was improved.
Competitive Analysis
Gaps were found in direct and indirect competitors within the sector.
Heuristic Evaluation
To understand the problems in the core experience and the system, a heuristic evaluation was performed.

Generating solutions

Problem statement &User stories
After defining the Problem statement, User stories were made to help me in the generation of user-centered ideas.
Task flows
Task Flows of the main user actions on the site were created.
Sitemap
The main content of the site was organized in a sitemap.
